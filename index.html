Currently, I am an Assistant Professor of Department of Mathematics at New York City College of Technology.
I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the University of Houston under the supervision of Dr. Tsorng-Whay Pan in summer 2017. After graduated, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Houston collaborating with Dr. Tsorng-Whay Pan and Dr. Roland Glowinski in 2018. After Houston, I was a postdoctoral scholar of Scientific Computing Department working with Dr. Bryan Quaife at Florida State University. In 2019, I was a Postdoctoral Research Associate of Department of Mathematical Sciences working with Dr. Enkeleida Lushi at New Jersey Institute of Technology . In 2021, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow of Department of Mathematical, Physical, and Engineering Sciences at Texas A&M-San Antonio. In 2022, I was a C.-C. Hsiung Visiting Assistant Professor of Department of Mathematics working with Dr. Yue Yu at Lehigh University
